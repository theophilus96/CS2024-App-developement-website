Trace Together App

TraceTogether supports Singapore’s efforts to fight the spread of COVID-19 through community-driven contact tracing. TraceTogether notifies you quickly if you’ve been exposed to COVID-19 through close contact with other TraceTogether users.

Key Features:

  • The application is simple and easy-to-use. Simply download the app, grant the app permission to log data and turn on your Bluetooth.
  • The mobile app uses Bluetooth signals to determine if you are near another TraceTogether user. No location data is collected.
  • The data logged by the app remains encrypted in the mobile device where it is installed at all times. Only those who test positive for COVID-19 will be asked to share the data logged to speed up contact tracing.

SG Secure App

SGSecure is a national movement to sensitise, train and mobilise Singaporeans to play a part to prevent and deal with terrorist attacks.

Key Features:

  • Report: Where users send in information on suspicious incidents or objects.
  • Emergency Call: Which allows the user to call the police at 999, send an SMS to the police, or call emergency services at 995.
  • Alerts: Where user check news flashes about emergencies. By tapping on the map icon on the top right of the screen, user can see where the event is happening.
  • Get Ready: Information about the SGSecure movement, and tips on what to do in the event of an attack or crisis

LifeSG App

LifeSG (previously Moments of Life) gives you simpler access to government digital services and allows you to handle tasks with ease.

More than 40 government services and information have been personalised and consolidated into just one app. Check off your to-do list, manage your applications, keep track of the latest government schemes and benefits.

Key Features:

  • Apply for birth registration and baby bonus online
  • Search for preschools and indicate interest for your child
  • Explore active ageing programmes and senior’s discounts
  • Find job opportunities, skills upgrading courses and job support
  • Stay updated with the latest government schemes, benefits and events
