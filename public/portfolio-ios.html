WorkOutDoors

The only configurable workout app for the Apple Watch that displays a detailed vector map, with over 300 live metrics, graphs and buttons that can be displayed in many different screen layouts.

MAP FEATURES:

  • GPX routes can be imported and displayed on the map
  • routes are coloured in varying shades of purple: red for uphill; blue for downhill
  • a breadcrumb trail of your route
  • the map can be panned with your finger, and zoomed with the digital crown

Workout Features:

  • configure from a pool of over 300 live metrics
  • configure interval schedules (e.g. warm-up, run fast then rest x 10, cooldown)
  • manual or configurable automatic laps (e.g. every mile or km)
  • individually configurable rolling pace fields (e.g last mile, last minute etc)
  • 28 different activity types
  • Graphical data fields: historical graphs, bar charts, trail maps & more
  • "Now Playing" music controls now available from a button within the app

Notability

Welcome to Notability: powerful, yet wonderfully simple note-taking and PDF annotation. Apple Editors' Choice on iPad, iPhone, and Mac! Top selling paid app on iPad App Store, regularly ranked #1.

Students, teachers, and business professionals use Notability daily to enrich their lives. It is uniquely designed for each device to provide the best note-taking experience at school, home, and work.

In Notability, you can:

  • Work with a variety of note-taking, journaling, and drawing tools like ink, highlighter, text, and audio.
  • Add photos, GIFs, web pages, and more.
  • Group notes with customizable subjects and dividers.
  • Secure notes using Face ID and Touch ID.
  • Ink is fine-tuned for the Apple Pencil for the most responsive, precise writing experience.
  • Use iCloud to automatically sync your notes across iPad, iPhone, and Mac.
